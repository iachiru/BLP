Seinen

Seinen animes are stories targeted to young adult men. They usually contain action scenes and a complicated storyline that will make the viewer reflect on specific social conventions. Despite the target, they are incredibly popular among young adults of all genders due to their complexity and the situations they present.

Shonen

Shonen animes are stories targeting young boys. They focus mainly on action stories and overcoming tragic circumstances. This genre is one of the most extended and liked in western countries

Shojo

Shojo animes are the female counterpart of shonen so their target is mainly young girls. They tipically focus on relationship stories, be it frienship or romance. This genre is widely extended and enjoyed.

Sports anime

Sports anime, as the name indicates are stories built around some kind of sport. This is one of the many genres manga and anime encompass. There is probably an anime genre for any taste. These stories are mainly motivational and expose struggles proffessional athletes face on a daily basis. They are usually very accurate and descriptive in regards of the sport depicted, however, sometimes they might include fantasy elements.
